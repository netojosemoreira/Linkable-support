Getting Started

How do I save a link?
You can save links in two ways. Inside the app, tap the + button on the Links tab and paste or type a URL. From any other app (Safari, Chrome, etc.), tap the Share button and choose Linkable from the share sheet to save instantly without leaving what you're reading.
How do I use the Share Extension?
While browsing in Safari or any other browser, tap the share icon, scroll through the app list, and tap Linkable. The link will be saved directly to your library. If you don't see Linkable, tap More and enable it from the list of extensions.
How do I organise links with tags?
Open any saved link and tap Edit, then add one or more tags. You can browse all your tags from the Tags tab to quickly filter your library by topic.

Reading & Viewing

What is Reader mode?
Reader mode strips away ads and clutter from an article, showing just the text and images in a clean, easy-to-read layout. You can set it as your default view in Settings → Preferred View → Reader.
Can I set a default view for links?
Yes. Go to Settings → Preferred View and choose between Online (full web page) or Reader (distraction-free article view). This applies to all links you open.

Linkable Pro

What is Linkable Pro?
Linkable Pro removes all banner ads from the app, giving you a completely clean experience. You can upgrade from the Settings tab — look for the upgrade banner.
How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Go to Settings, tap the upgrade banner, then tap Restore Purchases at the bottom of the upgrade screen. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID, so make sure you're signed in with the same account you used when you originally bought it.
I was charged but the app still shows ads. What do I do?
First try restoring your purchase (see above). If the issue persists, please contact us with your Apple ID and approximate purchase date and we'll sort it out promptly.

Troubleshooting

A link isn't loading correctly. What can I do?
Try switching between Online and Reader mode using the toolbar at the bottom of the link view. If the page still won't load, check your internet connection, or try opening the link in Safari via the share icon. If the problem persists, let us know which URL is causing trouble.
The app crashed or something isn't working.
Please try closing and relaunching the app first. If the issue continues, update to the latest version of Linkable from the App Store. Still stuck? Send us a message with a description of what happened and your iOS version, and we'll investigate.
